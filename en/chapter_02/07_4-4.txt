It is activated from the Convoy Window and is the fourth icon in that Window's title bar (an eye).</p><p>Click on the vehicle already in circulation so that the Convoy Window is displayed. Look for the fourth icon in the title bar of the Convoy Window (eye icon) and press it to follow the convoy.</p><p><st>The tutorial will move to the next step</st> when you are following the convoy.</p>
